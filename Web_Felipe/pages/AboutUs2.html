Snowboarding

No list would be complete without Tremblant. And what we love about this spot is that there are far more activities that just skiing and snowboarding, so it’s a great choice for families and groups where not everyone skis. First, the skiing. Mont Tremblant boasts “the largest and most varied skiable terrain in Eastern North America.” With 47 of their 96 trails classified as “very difficult and extreme”, you can spend days discovering the mountain without ever getting bored.
